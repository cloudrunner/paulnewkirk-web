Services

What I Do

Solo Guitar & Vocals

I have performed as a solo guitarist and vocalist for more than 30 years. During this time I've played multi-year engagements at several Atlanta venues, including Dante's Down the Hatch, Hollihan's, Basils and many others. I have a large repertoire of jazz, pop, rock, and country music, and can provide a setlist tailored to your event.

Duo & Trio Performances

Smaller groups are often a great balance of sound, flexibility, and budget. Available configurations include:

  • Jazz trio - Piano, bass drums (listen here)
  • Guitar, bass and vocals
  • Steel pan and Island music
  • Piano, bass and vocals

I also perform regularly with jazz trios throughout Atlanta, including more than 30 years with the Atlanta Jazz Trio (listen here).

Larger Groups

For events that call for a bigger sound, I can provide several ensemble options:

Atlanta Jazz Quartet

  • Available with our vocalist or saxophonist
  • Ideal for receptions, corporate events, and private parties

The Atlanta Crawdaddies

  • A New Orleans-inspired group featuring 1–4 horns
  • Repertoire includes jazz, blues, R&B, classic rock, and some country
  • Learn more here

On the Day of Your Event

I perform at many events every year, but on the day of your event, that's my focus. I'm meticulous about preparation, so you can expect:

  • Arrival and setup with plenty of time to spare
  • Time built in for traffic, sound checks, and last-minute adjustments
  • Backup keyboard, speaker, and cables on-site
  • Backup copies of any special music you've requested

My goal is simple: provide great music and make sure everything runs smoothly.

About

About Paul

Paul Newkirk Paul provides quality entertainment for corporate and private events looking for gentle background music or an entire one man show with vocals.

"Jazz is the foundation — but the music goes wherever the room needs it to go."

A regular performer at metro Atlanta restaurants, Paul excels at Jimmy Buffet, James Taylor, The Eagles and more. A sure crowd-pleaser, Paul’s smooth vocals and large and diverse repertoire provide the perfect environment for enjoyable entertaining.

Based in Atlanta and available throughout Georgia, the Southeast, and nationally for destination events and special occasions. Solo guitar/vocals, duo, and ensemble configurations all available.
